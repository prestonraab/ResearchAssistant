DISCREPANCIES:
==============

1. AFRICA (GSE79362 - Zak et al. 2016):
   Manuscript: 153 samples (46 progressors, 107 non-progressors)
   Actual:     181 samples (77 active TB, 104 latent TB)
   Issue:      Different sample count AND different labels
               - Manuscript describes progression study
               - Actual data uses active/latent classification
               - 181 samples suggests using ALL samples, not just progressors

2. INDIA:
   Manuscript: GSE101705, 44 samples (28 active, 16 latent) - Leong et al. 2018
   Actual:     GSE107994, 103 samples (53 active, 50 latent) - Leong et al. 2018
   Issue:      WRONG GEO ACCESSION in manuscript
               - Should be GSE107994, not GSE101705
               - Using Leicester UK cohort (South Asian descent)
               - Much larger sample size than manuscript states

3. GSE39941 (Anderson et al. 2014):
   Manuscript: 334 samples (111 active, 54 latent, 169 other)
   Actual:     70 samples (20 active, 50 latent) - Malawi subset only
   Issue:      Using filtered subset
               - HIV-negative only
               - Malawi region only (not SA/Kenya)
               - Removed 'other' disease states
               - Much smaller than full study

4. GSE37250 (Kaforou et al. 2013):
   Manuscript: 584 samples (195 active, 167 latent, 222 other)
   Actual:     180 samples total, split into:
               - GSE37250_SA: 94 samples (46 active, 48 latent)
               - GSE37250_M:  86 samples (51 active, 35 latent)
   Issue:      Using filtered subsets
               - HIV-negative only
               - Split by region (SA vs Malawi)
               - Removed 'other' disease states
               - Much smaller than full study

5. USA:
   Manuscript: GSE19491/GSE42834, 440 samples (61 active, 69 latent, 310 other)
   Actual:     GSE73408, 70 samples (35 active, 35 latent)
   Issue:      COMPLETELY DIFFERENT STUDY
               - Manuscript lists GSE19491/GSE42834
               - Actually using GSE73408 (Walter et al. 2016)
               - Different sample counts

6. MISSING FROM ACTUAL DATA:
